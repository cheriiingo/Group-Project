Excelsior School

Uniform for Girls

Winter Size

In Excelsior School, we have our own uniforms. Now, let's talk about the uniforms of girls. Girls have jackets and scarfs for winter uniforms. The jackets are in color grey, there is a logo on the left. The logo looks like a shield, there are four things in the shield, such as bear, Dr. hat, lab instrument and book. Under the shield, there is a mark and it is written down Excelsior. Also, we have yellow scarfs. However, the weather in California is not very cold, so, for most of time, we don't need to wear it. there is a logo on the scarf too. For the scarf, we can use it to cover our necks, we also can put it over the head, then it will be the headkerchief. that's a dual-use.

The jacket is very warm, but it is easy to make static electricity. Not only students can wear the uniforms, but also teachers. we have all kinds of size for the uniforms. just get what you want.
